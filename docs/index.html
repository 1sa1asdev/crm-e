crm-e

A free, open-source desktop CRM for job seekers. Track applications, send emails, and stay organised — all from one place.

Download View on GitHub

Everything you need to manage your job search

Built with Tauri — lightweight, fast, and runs entirely on your machine.

Application tracking

Track every application with status, contact info, dates, and notes in one clean table.

Email integration

Connect Gmail or Outlook and send templated emails directly from the app. Replies are synced automatically.

Email templates

Create reusable email templates with dynamic placeholders for company, role, and your profile details.

Cover letters

Write cover letter templates with a live PDF preview. Generate and attach a PDF when composing.

Lead tracking

Keep a list of contacts and recruiters separate from your active applications.

AI email analysis

Use OpenRouter to analyse your email threads and get suggested status updates automatically.
